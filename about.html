About

I am a Computer Science student at Ashoka University with a minor in Biology and Psychology. My work sits between neuroethology, computational neuroscience, scientific imaging, and tool-building for research workflows.

Across academic and lab settings, I have worked on automated zebrafish movement analysis, electrophysiology workflows, microscopy-heavy biology questions, and mathematical modelling for neural systems. I am especially interested in building interfaces and analysis tools that make experimental work more legible and reproducible.
