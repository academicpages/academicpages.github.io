I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science in the [Khoury College of Computer Sciences](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu) at [Northeastern University](https://www.northeastern.edu). I am working in [The Helping Hands Lab](https://www2.ccs.neu.edu/research/helpinghands/), advised by Professor [Robert Platt](http://www.ccs.neu.edu/home/rplatt/) and Professor [Robin Walters](http://mathserver.neu.edu/robin/).

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in [Sichuan University](http://www.scu.edu.cn), Chengdu, China.

Email: wang dot dian at northeastern dot edu
