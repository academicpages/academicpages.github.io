## üìä Selected Projects

### **CEO Interview Sentiment & Event Analysis (INSEAD, Singapore)**  
Developed an end-to-end pipeline for **3,957 CNBC interview transcripts** (26M+ words).  
Extracted CEO-only speaking segments, applied the LM dictionary, and designed event detectors. Identified systematic sentiment differences across programs.

### **American Option Pricing via LSMC**  
Implemented a refined Longstaff‚ÄìSchwartz Monte Carlo model with Laguerre basis functions.  
Performed sensitivity analyses on volatility, discounting, and dividends to study early-exercise incentives.

### **Algorithmic Trading: Fair Value Gap Strategy**  
Designed a multi-timeframe strategy for Nasdaq-100 futures.  
Achieved **6% ROI in two weeks** in controlled backtesting with strict risk management.

### **Mortgage Default Risk Modeling**  
Processed **5M+ Fannie Mae loans** and benchmarked multiple ML models for RMBS default prediction.  
Delivered >92% accuracy with strong specificity.
