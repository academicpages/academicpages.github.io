About Me
------
I've had an interest in working with and analyzing data since I was a kid. I've worked in data science and analytics for 8+ years now. I went to college at Northeastern University (go Huskies!) and studied economics and mathematics. After that, I worked for 5 years in credit risk. In 2021, I took the leap of going back to school to get my Master's in statistics at NC State (go Pack!) - I learned a lot and really enjoyed the applied statistics projects I got to work on. Clearly I like schools with wolf/husky mascots. Now I work on customer churn and lifetime value models and analysis stemming from those.

Outside of work, I like board games, music, skiing, and Seattle sports (plus some other teams sprinkled in). I'm trying to write about and highlight some of my side projects, most of which are related to sports.
