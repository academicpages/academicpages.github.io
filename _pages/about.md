## About Me

I am a third-year PhD candidate at the **ETH Zurich Robotics Systems Lab**. My research focuses on **robot learning and control**, with particular interests in **Reinforcement Learning for Legged Locomotion**, **Sim-to-Real Transfer**, and **Dexterous Manipulation**.

## Education

* **Ph.D. in Robotics** (2021-Present) — ETH Zurich
* **M.Sc.** (2019-2021) — Technical University of Munich (TUM), graduated September 2021
* **B.Sc.** — Technical University of Munich (TUM)

## Research Experience
