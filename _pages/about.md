Nancy Lau is a 2nd year Computer Science PhD student at UC Santa Cruz. Currently a project lead on creating a cyberdefense coding eval for frontier AI models through [Supervised Program for Alignment Research (SPAR)](https://sparai.org/). Her work has been supported by GPU compute credits from Prime Intellect, Lium.io, and Modal.
