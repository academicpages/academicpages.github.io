I am a Computer Science student at Ashoka University with a minor in Biology and Psychology. My research interests lie at the intersection of neuroscience, biology, and computer science.

I have research experience in:
- **Evolutionary Neuroscience & Developmental Self-Organization**: At EMBL Heidelberg.
- **Neural Systems & Electrophysiology**: At Hyderabad Central University.
- **Neuro-ethology & Behavioral Analysis**: At Ashoka University.
- **Mathematical Biology & Knot Theory**: Through the Math Apprenticeship Program at Ashoka University.

I enjoy building tools for scientific analysis, such as automated behavioral tracking pipelines and high-performance disk analysis tools. I am proficient in Python, Rust, and specialized neuro-scientific software/libraries.
