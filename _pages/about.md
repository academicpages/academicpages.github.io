I am a first-year PhD candidate at the [HKUST NLP Group](https://nlp.hkust.edu.hk/), supervised by [Professor Junxian He](https://junxianhe.com/). I graduated from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) in June 2024 with a B.Eng. degree.

Research Interests
======
My research focuses on natural language processing and machine learning, with specific interests in:
- LLM Reasoning and Reinforcement Learning
- Hallucination in Vision-Language Models (VLM)
- LLM Truthfulness and Interpretability
