What I'm working on

I'm currently working as a data analyst at the [Center for Brain Circuit Therpeutics](https://www.brighamandwomens.org/neurosciences-center/center-for-brain-circuit-therapeutics) at [Brigham and Women's Hospital](https://www.brighamandwomens.org/).

At the center, I'm in charge of the Center's computational infrastructure and computational methods development. In addition to supporting research at the Center, I am also investigating new ways to think about lesion network mapping and other types of functional connectivity analyses. In particular, I am developing a set of new tools involving what I call the Precomputed Functional Connectome, a high-resolution voxel-wise atlas of functional connectivity in the brain. Check out what I'm working on [here](https://github.com/thewilliamdrew/pfc-toolkit).
