This WebGIS system supports environmental monitoring visualization and environmental social risk analysis. The project covered requirement analysis, feature design, frontend and backend development, testing, documentation, and delivery.

Role: Full-stack Developer

Time: 2021.10 - 2022.03

Tech stack: Vue2, AMap API, Flask, PostgreSQL, PostGIS

Key work:
- Designed and implemented the main workflow: study area selection, buffer generation, risk/POI analysis, and result export.
- Supported online drawing, coordinate input, address/POI search, administrative region selection, and vector upload.
- Implemented buffer analysis, raster clipping, multi-layer overlay, indicator weight configuration, statistical export, and visualization for 12 risk indicators.
- Prepared user manuals and operation documents, and supported testing and delivery.
