This research investigates biomimetic design strategies inspired by tafoni rock formations to create porous architectural spaces. The work demonstrates how computational design can translate natural patterns into architectural applications.

Recommended citation: Ye, W., Chen, S., Zhao, X., Xu, W. (2022). "Porous Space — Biomimetic of Tafoni in Computational Design." <i>Architectural Intelligence</i>, 18 (2022).
