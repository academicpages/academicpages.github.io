excerpt: 'This paper proposes the first evaluation of NMT in the Spanish-Korean language pair. Four types of human evaluation —Direct Assessment, Ranking Comparison and MT Post-Editing(MTPE) time/effort— and one semi-automatic methods are applied. The NMT engine is represented by Google Translate in newswire domain. After assessed by six professional translators, the engine demonstrates 78% of performance and 37% productivity gain in MTPE. Additionally, 40.249% of the outputs of the engine are modified with an interval of 15 months, showing 11% of progress rate.'
